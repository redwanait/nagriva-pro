What Is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization

SEO is the strategic practice of increasing a website's visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs). It encompasses a broad range of techniques — from keyword research and on-page content optimization to technical infrastructure and authority building — all designed to attract qualified organic traffic.

On-page optimization — content, meta tags, and internal linking
Technical SEO — site speed, mobile-friendliness, and structured data
Off-page SEO — backlinks, authority signals, and brand mentions
