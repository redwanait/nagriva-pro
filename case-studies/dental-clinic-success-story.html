Project Overview

Modernizing a Dental Clinic's Digital Presence

A well-established dental clinic in Casablanca was struggling to attract new patients online. Their existing website was outdated, slow, and not optimized for mobile devices — resulting in missed opportunities and a declining appointment booking rate.

The clinic partnered with Nagriva to completely redesign their online presence. Our goal was to create a modern, trustworthy, and conversion-focused website that would make it easy for potential patients to find the clinic, learn about services, and book appointments.

The Challenge

What Was Holding The Clinic Back

The clinic was losing potential patients every day due to fundamental issues with their old website.

Outdated Website

The old design looked unprofessional and failed to inspire trust in potential patients.

Poor Mobile Experience

Over 60% of visitors accessed the site on mobile, but the experience was broken and frustrating.

Difficult Appointment Booking

Patients had to call during office hours — there was no online booking or WhatsApp option available.

Low Patient Trust

Missing testimonials, before/after galleries, and credentials made the clinic appear less credible.

Six-Pronged Approach to Growth

We designed a premium, patient-focused website that reflects the quality of care the clinic provides. Every element was crafted to build trust and encourage appointment bookings.

The site was built mobile-first, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices. We integrated an online booking system and WhatsApp chat so patients could schedule appointments instantly, 24/7.

Local SEO optimization and performance improvements ensured the clinic ranks higher in search results and loads in under 2 seconds.
