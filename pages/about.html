Our Mission

Helping People Discover New Career Opportunities

Nagriva is a career platform focused on helping people discover job opportunities and access useful career resources. Our mission is to simplify the job search process and make relevant opportunities easier to find.

From job listings and internships to career insights and professional resources, we aim to provide practical tools that help individuals take the next step in their careers.

✦ A Message From The Founder

Making Career Opportunities Easier to Discover

I started Nagriva with a simple goal: to make career opportunities easier to discover and access. Finding relevant jobs, internships, and career resources can often be time-consuming. Nagriva was created to bring these opportunities together in one place, helping people stay informed and explore options that match their interests and goals.

Our mission is to provide a reliable platform where job seekers can discover opportunities, access useful resources, and take the next step in their professional journey.

As Nagriva continues to grow, we remain focused on creating practical tools and content that help people navigate their careers with greater confidence.

Redouane Ait El Hadj
Founder & CEO, Nagriva
