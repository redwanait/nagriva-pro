ROI Calculator FAQ

Common questions about calculating return on investment.

ROI is calculated as ((Revenue − Investment) ÷ Investment) × 100. This gives you the percentage return on your investment. A positive ROI means you earned more than you spent.
A 5:1 ratio (500% ROI) is generally considered excellent. 2:1 (200%) is good, and anything above 1:1 (100%) means you're profiting. Industry benchmarks vary — digital marketing often targets 300-500% ROI.
Include all direct costs: ad spend, software/tools, labor hours, creative production, agency fees, and any overhead directly attributable to the campaign. The more accurate your costs, the more reliable your ROI calculation.
